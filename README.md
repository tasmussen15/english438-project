A text-based Choose Your Own Adventure game by Thomas Asmussen

## Story

You play as Emily St. Aubert, a recently orphaned child of a landed rural French
family. Your guardianship has fallen to your inaffectionate aunt, Madame Cheron.
She separates you from your beloved Valancourt, a handsome gentleman of good
status, and then marries Montoni, the scheming Italian nobleman. Montoni forces
you and your aunt to move to his mysterious castle: the Castle of Udolpho. Here
he reveals his true character as a villainous leader of bandits and imprisons
your aunt, ultimately leading to her unfortunate death.

With your aunt deceased, it's only a matter of time before Montoni decides to
get rid of you, too. That is why tonight, you and your faithful servant,
Annette, will brave the wrath of Montoni and the schemes of your kidnapping
suitor Count Morano to attempt to make your escape from the Castle of Udolpho.

Based on the novel *The Mysteries of Udolpho* by Ann Radcliffe.
